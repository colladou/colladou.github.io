Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a PhD Candidate at University of California Irvine (UCI) in the Computer Science Department. I work with Professor Pierre Baldi researching new methods for machine learning in high energy physics. My current projects focus on improving and understanding b-tagging for the Higgs Boson at the Large Hadron Collider using deep learning. I am also interested in hyper-parameter optimization algorithms and adversarial samples.

Before pursuing a PhD I did a BS in Physics at the University of Costa Rica.
